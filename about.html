Lift Off

There are definitely a few athletes who are overly analytical about their training. A log book can be cluttered with too much analysis and detail. Be careful of this! The classic saying, "Paralysis by analysis" is to be avoided if possible! Remember the primary reason you are training is for enjoyment and relaxation, and of course for the results. Use this journal with these central feelings in mind, so the journal does not get you bogged down.
